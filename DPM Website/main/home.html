Welcome to Mangaung Dance Perfomance website

This website records and celebrates the diverse dance heritage of the Mangaung community. By documenting historical roots, cultural meanings, and artistic expressions, the project aims to support cultural preservation, academic research and community engagement.

Focus

We have selected three dance forms for in depth study:

  • Mokgibo
  • Umxhentso
  • Indlamu
  • Setapa

Purpose

The site aims to:
(1) Document the evolution of each dance form,
(2) Illustrate its role in community rituals and social life, and
(3) Provide a multimedia showcase that can be used for education, tourism promotion, and further scholarly inquiry.
